Artist profile

Shinto
Okukawa

Character Artist

He started his career in Digital since 2013 with excellence in Smartphone Games & Mobile Advertising in Japan. In 2016, Shinto was assigned as the developer of the Character Licensing & Marketing Solutions business model in the Thai market.
In 2020, he officially assumed the position of Managing Director of the branch in Vietnam, a market that Minto realizes has great potential for the group's business areas.
What sets Shinto apart is their ability to delve deep into the human experience and convey complex emotions through their artwork. They have a keen eye for capturing raw and vulnerable moments, infusing their pieces with a sense of intimacy and introspection. Whether it's a thought-provoking portrait, a breathtaking landscape, or an abstract composition, Shinto has an innate ability to connect with viewers on a profound level.
