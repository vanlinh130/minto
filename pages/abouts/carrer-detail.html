Job description
FULL TIME ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT INTERNSHIP | HYBRID
What you’ll do
  • Assist in the implementation, and monitoring of marketing campaigns and initiatives for clients.
  • Assist in approaching new clients to consult company services.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including design, content, and social media, to ensure project milestones are met.
  • Help create project schedules and timelines, and track progress against them.
  • Assist in the management of project budgets and resource allocation.
  • Help monitor and report on the performance of client campaigns and initiatives.
  • Provide general administrative support as needed.
Qualifications:
  • Strong passion for Characters content is a plus.
  • Honesty, positive attitude and eagerness to learn.
  • Currently pursuing or recently graduated with a degree in project management, marketing, or a related field.
  • Strong organizational and time management skills.
  • Ability to multitask and manage multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Good communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Detail-oriented and able to identify potential issues before they arise.
  • Comfortable with English communication.
Benefits
  • Internship salary 3 million per month
  • Hybrid position
  • Work from Monday - Friday
  • Open to be considered for trainee position after 3 months if your performance is well
